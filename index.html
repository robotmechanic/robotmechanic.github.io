Who am I?

I am a game developer living in Tokyo, Japan. I specialize in creating 3D games in Unity. My long experience in the CG and software industry has given me the skills to plan, design and program a high quality 3D game efficiently for a fraction of the cost larger studios require. Currently developing new game projects using Unity and exploring new opportunities for iOS/Android and PC/Mac platforms.

I am also a Maya character rigger and modeler with over 10 years of industry experience in the field of high end commercials, interactive, games, television and film visual effects. Current Projects

Introduction

Galaxica is an procedurally generated action (roguelike?) game set in space. This is a game that I started working since last December. I wanted to polish a working prototype to show the community (this means you) and to get feedback on what you like, hate, etc. I've failed plenty of times at my personal game releases in the past I really want to gauge the communities interests and reaction before investing further time into it.

The game in a nutshell Travel interstellar distances in your starship in search of rare materials you can use to trade. Fight alien races and space pirates and take their stuff. Use said stuff to purchase upgrades from the local vendor to increase your chances of survival. Explore randomly generated 3D worlds by landing on planets and disembarking and mining/harvesting the planet for its wealth. Dungeon exploration on planets and on ships
